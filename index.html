You've Been Phished!

This was a training exercise.

You just scanned a QR code without knowing where it would take you. In a real attack, this could have:

  • Downloaded malware to your device
  • Stolen your login credentials
  • Taken you to a fake website
  • Accessed your camera or location
🛡️ Stay Safe: QR code attacks are on the rise! Never scan QR codes from unknown sources, posters in public places, or unsolicited emails. Always verify the source first!
